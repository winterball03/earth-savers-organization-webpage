  • Key Facts:

  • 1. Diameter: Approximately 12,742 kilometers (7,918 miles)
  • 2. Surface Area: 510.1 million square kilometers (196.9 million square miles)
  • 3. Population: Over 7.9 billion people as of 2024
  • 4. Continents: Seven main continents - Africa, Antarctica, Asia, Europe, North America, Australia (Oceania), and South America
  • 5. Oceans: Five major oceans - Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Southern (Antarctic), and Arctic

  • Features and Landforms:

  • Earth is characterized by a diverse range of features and landforms, including:
  • 1. Mountains: The tallest mountain on Earth is Mount Everest, located in the Himalayas, with a peak reaching 8,848 meters (29,029 feet) above sea level.
  • 2. Oceans: Covering about 71% of Earth's surface, the oceans are vital for regulating the planet's climate and supporting a vast array of marine life.
  • 3. Forests: Earth is home to lush forests such as the Amazon Rainforest, which play a crucial role in oxygen production and biodiversity.
  • 4. Deserts: From the Sahara Desert in Africa to the Atacama Desert in South America, deserts are arid regions that showcase the resilience of life in extreme conditions.

WHO ARE WE?

Earth Savers is a dedicated organization working to ensure a healthy and sustainable future for our planet. We believe in empowering individuals and communities to take action and make a positive impact on the environment, with a particular focus on advocating for policies and practices that reduce plastic pollution and promote recycling initiatives.

Join us in preserving Earth's beauty and resources for generations to come!
